1. Introduction

Caipher AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered phone automation services and visit our website at caipherai.com.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name
  • Account Information: Username, password, billing address
  • Business Information: Business type, industry, scheduling preferences
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Call Data: Phone numbers, call duration, call recordings, transcripts, and metadata
  • Usage Data: Features used, appointment bookings, customer interactions
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies and Tracking: Website usage patterns and preferences

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Data from CRM integrations (Google Calendar, HubSpot, etc.)
  • Information from Twilio and ElevenLabs for call processing
  • Payment information from Stripe or other payment processors

3. SMS/Text Messaging Terms

Important: By providing your phone number and consenting to receive text messages from Caipher AI, you agree to the following terms regarding SMS/text messaging.

3.1 Types of Messages

Caipher AI may send you the following types of text messages:

  • Appointment Confirmations: Confirmation of scheduled demos or meetings
  • Appointment Reminders: Reminders about upcoming scheduled appointments
  • Follow-up Messages: Responses to your inquiries or demo requests
  • Scheduling Links: Links to book appointments with our team
  • Service Updates: Important updates about our services related to your account

3.2 Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with Caipher AI. Typically, you may receive 1-5 messages per interaction or appointment. We do not send unsolicited marketing messages or cold outreach.

3.3 How You Consent to Receive Messages

You consent to receive text messages from Caipher AI when you:

  • Call our business phone number and provide your contact information
  • Submit a demo request form on our website with your phone number
  • Request a callback or follow-up via our website or chatbot
  • Verbally consent during a phone call to receive a text message
  • Reply START or YES to opt-in to messaging

By providing your phone number through any of these methods and requesting communication, you expressly consent to receive text messages from Caipher AI related to your inquiry or appointment.

3.4 Opting Out of Text Messages

You can opt out of receiving text messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP: Text "STOP" to any message from Caipher AI to immediately unsubscribe
  • Contacting Support: Email support@caipherai.com to request removal from messaging

After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation message. You will not receive any further messages unless you opt back in by texting START.

3.5 Help and Support

For assistance with our text messaging service:

  • Reply HELP to any message for support information
  • Email: support@caipherai.com
  • Phone: (762) 224-7437

3.6 Costs and Carrier Fees

Message and data rates may apply. Standard messaging fees from your mobile carrier may apply to messages sent and received. Caipher AI does not charge for text messages, but your carrier's standard messaging rates will apply.

3.7 Carrier Disclaimer

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Message delivery is subject to effective transmission from your network operator.

3.8 No Sharing of Mobile Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or any mobile messaging data with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Your phone number is used exclusively for the purposes described in this policy: appointment confirmations, reminders, and responses to your inquiries. We will never share your mobile information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We use your information for the following purposes:
