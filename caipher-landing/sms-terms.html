What Messages Will You Receive?

When you opt in to receive text messages from Caipher AI, you may receive the following types of messages:

  • Appointment Confirmations: Confirmation of scheduled demos, meetings, or service appointments
  • Appointment Reminders: Reminders about upcoming scheduled appointments
  • Follow-Up Messages: Responses to your inquiries or demo requests
  • Scheduling Links: Links to book or reschedule appointments with our team
  • Service Updates: Important updates about services related to your account

Caipher AI does not send unsolicited marketing messages, cold outreach, or promotional spam. All messages are transactional and conversational, directly related to your inquiry or appointment.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with Caipher AI. You may receive approximately 1-5 messages per interaction or appointment. Recurring messages may be sent for appointment reminders and follow-ups.

How to Opt In (Call to Action)

You consent to receive text messages from Caipher AI when you voluntarily take any of the following actions:

Opt-In Methods

1. Website Form Submission: When you submit a demo request, contact form, or booking form on caipherai.com and provide your phone number, you consent to receive appointment-related text messages.

2. Calendly Booking: When you schedule a demo or meeting through our Calendly booking page (linked from caipherai.com) and provide your phone number, you consent to receive appointment confirmations and reminders via text.

3. Phone Call Opt-In: When you call our business phone number at (762) 224-7437 and verbally provide your phone number or request to receive a text message, you consent to receive text messages related to your inquiry.

4. Text-In Opt-In: When you text START, YES, or SUBSCRIBE to our business number, you consent to receive messages from Caipher AI.

5. Chatbot Interaction: When you interact with our website chatbot and provide your phone number requesting follow-up, you consent to receive text messages.

By providing your phone number through any of the above methods and requesting communication, you expressly consent to receive text messages from Caipher AI related to your inquiry, appointment, or account.

Consent is not required as a condition of purchase. You are not required to opt in to SMS messaging to use Caipher AI's services or purchase any product.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time using any of the following methods:

  • Reply STOP: Text "STOP" to any message from Caipher AI to immediately unsubscribe from all future messages
  • Reply CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, END, or QUIT: Any of these keywords will also opt you out
  • Contact Support: Email support@caipherai.com or call (762) 224-7437 to request removal

After opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message: "You have been unsubscribed from Caipher AI messages. You will not receive any more messages. Reply START to re-subscribe."

You will not receive any further messages unless you opt back in by texting START.
