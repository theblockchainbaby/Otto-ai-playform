What the AI Handles

Your AI receptionist manages routine calls so your staff can focus on patient care.

Appointment Management

Books, reschedules, and cancels appointments based on your availability rules and provider calendars. Syncs directly with your scheduling system.

Patient Intake

Collects structured information before the visit — name, DOB, reason for visit, insurance status, and symptom categories — saving staff time.

Smart Triage Routing

Identifies urgent vs routine cases and routes appropriately — emergency guidance, nurse callback queue, or standard scheduling.

Insurance & Billing FAQs

Answers common questions about accepted insurance plans, payment methods, visit types, and billing procedures based on your approved responses.

After-Hours Coverage

Captures messages, schedules future callbacks, or escalates urgent matters based on your clinic's rules — even at 2 AM.

Call Routing

Transfers calls to the right department — billing, nurse line, scheduling desk, or specific providers — when human help is needed.

How the Medical Workflow Operates

A step-by-step look at how every patient call is handled.

1

Call Answered Instantly

AI greets the patient, identifies your clinic by name, and asks how it can help. No hold times, no missed calls.

2

Intent Detection

The system identifies the purpose of the call:

  • Book a new appointment
  • Change or cancel existing appointment
  • Symptoms or health concern
  • Billing or insurance question
  • Prescription inquiry
  • Other / General question
3

Patient Identification

The AI collects or confirms key patient information:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Phone number
  • New or existing patient status
4

Smart Path Routing

Based on intent, the AI follows the appropriate workflow path:

If Booking
  • Collects visit reason
  • Checks provider rules
  • Offers available time slots
  • Confirms appointment
If Symptoms
  • Asks triage questions
  • Flags severity level
  • Schedules urgent slot OR
  • Nurse callback / Emergency
If Billing
  • Provides FAQ responses
  • Answers insurance questions
  • Routes to billing desk
  • If needed
5

Data Structured & Logged

All information is automatically saved to:

  • Your CRM / EHR integration
  • Front desk notification queue
  • Complete call summary with transcript
6

Staff Handoff (Only When Needed)

Your staff only handles what requires human judgment:

  • Complex clinical questions
  • Escalated patient concerns
  • Clinical decisions

Routine calls never reach your front desk.

Example Patient Call Flows

See exactly how the AI handles common patient scenarios.

Appointment Booking Standard Flow

AI

Thank you for calling [Clinic Name]. How can I help you today?

Patient

I need to schedule an appointment.

AI

I can help with that. Are you a new or existing patient?

Patient

Existing.

AI

Great. May I have your full name and date of birth?

Patient

[Provides information]

AI

Thank you. What is the main reason for your visit today?

Patient

Ongoing knee pain.

AI

Understood. Is this pain severe, sudden, or accompanied by swelling or fever?

Patient

No.

AI

Thank you. I can schedule you with a provider. Here are the next available options: Tuesday at 10:30 AM or Thursday at 2:15 PM. Which works best?

Patient

Thursday.

AI

You're scheduled for Thursday at 2:15 PM. You'll receive a confirmation by text. Is there anything else I can help with?

Urgent Triage Emergency Protocol

AI

Are you experiencing chest pain, difficulty breathing, or a life-threatening emergency?

Patient

Yes, I'm having chest pain.

AI

Please hang up and call 911 immediately. If you'd like, I can notify the clinic of your call so they can follow up. Would you like me to do that?

Patient

Yes, please.

AI

I've flagged this as urgent and notified the clinic. Please call 911 now. Take care.

Emergency Protocol Active

The AI never attempts to handle emergencies. It immediately directs patients to 911 while offering to notify your clinic for follow-up.
