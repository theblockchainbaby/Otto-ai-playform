How the Workflow Operates

Every call follows a structured path.

1

Call Answered Instantly

AI greets the caller with your property name. No hold music, no voicemail.

2

Caller & Issue Identified

Determines if they're a tenant or prospect, and what they need help with.

3

Smart Path Routing

Maintenance → logs request. Leasing → schedules tour. Emergency → dispatches help.

4

Request Logged & Team Notified

All info pushed to your system. Team notified of urgent issues and new leads.
