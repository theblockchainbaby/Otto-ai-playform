How It Works

How the Workflow Operates

Every client call follows a structured path.

1

Call Answered Instantly

AI greets the caller, identifies your spa, and asks how it can help. No hold music.

2

Intent & Client Identification

Detects purpose (booking, pricing, questions) and collects name and contact info.

3

Smart Path Routing

Booking → offers time slots. Service questions → answers from your FAQ. Complex → transfers.

Data Logged & Staff Notified
