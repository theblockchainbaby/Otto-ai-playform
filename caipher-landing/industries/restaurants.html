How the Workflow Operates

Every call follows a structured path.

1

Call Answered Instantly

AI greets the caller with your restaurant name. No hold music during rush.

2

Intent Identified

Determines if they're booking a table, placing an order, or have questions.

3

Smart Path Routing

Reservation → books it. Takeout → takes order. Large party → logs for callback.

4

Order/Reservation Confirmed

Confirmation sent via text. Staff notified of new reservations and orders.
