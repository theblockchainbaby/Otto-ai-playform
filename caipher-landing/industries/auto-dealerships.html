What the AI Handles

Your AI receptionist manages routine calls so your sales and service teams can focus on customers.

Test Drive Scheduling

Books test drives based on vehicle availability and salesperson calendars. Sends confirmation texts.

Inventory Inquiries

Answers questions about vehicle availability, pricing, features, and specifications from your live inventory.

Service Appointments

Schedules oil changes, repairs, recalls, and maintenance. Routes complex issues to service advisors.

Financing Questions

Answers FAQs about financing options, credit requirements, and down payments. Captures lead info for F&I.

Trade-In Inquiries

Collects vehicle details for trade-in estimates. Routes hot leads to sales managers for follow-up.

After-Hours Leads

Captures buyer inquiries 24/7. Sends lead details to your CRM so sales can follow up first thing.

How the Workflow Operates

Every customer call follows a structured path.

1

Call Answered Instantly

AI greets the caller, identifies your dealership, and asks how it can help. No hold music.

2

Intent Detection

Identifies purpose — sales inquiry, service appointment, parts question, or financing — and routes accordingly.

3

Smart Information Gathering

Sales → vehicle preferences, budget, trade-in. Service → vehicle info, issue description, preferred time.

4

Action & Notification

Books appointments, answers FAQs, or creates leads in your CRM. Sales team notified of hot prospects instantly.
