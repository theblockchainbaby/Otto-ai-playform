Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the Otto dashboard?
Visit /otto-dashboard.html and login with your credentials. Demo login: john.anderson@otto.ai / admin123
How does Otto AI agent work?
Otto is a custom ElevenLabs conversational AI agent trained specifically for automotive customer service. It can handle calls, recognize customers, and provide intelligent responses.
Can I integrate Otto with my existing phone system?
Yes! Otto integrates with Twilio for phone calls. Check our API documentation for webhook endpoints and configuration details.
What automotive scenarios can Otto handle?
Otto can handle vehicle sales inquiries, service appointments, emergency roadside assistance, financing questions, and customer recognition for returning clients.
Is there a mobile app?
The Otto AI platform is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile devices through your web browser. A dedicated mobile app is planned for future release.
